HON. MINISTER OF FINANCE AND PLANNING, N MINISTRY OF FINANCE AND PLANNING,

COLOMBO.

Dear Sir,

In accordance with the provisions of Section 35 (1) of the Monetary Law

Act (Cap. 422,) I submit herewith the Thirty Fifth Annual Report of the

Monetary Board of the Central Bank of Ceylon.

Since 1975, a more detailed review of the economic trends and developments

in the various sectors of the economy has been published separately under the title

" Review of the Economy" . The review for 1984 will be published at an early

date.

I am, dear Sir,

Yours faithfully,

WARNASENA RASAPUTRA

Governor.

Central Bank of Ceylon,

Colombo.

30th April, 1985.
