HON. MINISTER O F FINANCE A N D PLANNING,

MINISTRY OF FINANCE A N D PLANNING,

COLOMBO.

Dear Sir,

In accordance with the provisions of Section 35(1) of the Monetary Law Act

(Cap. 422), I submit herewith the Thirty Sixth Annual Report of the Monetary

Board of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.

The Review of the Economy 1985 which is a more detailed assessment of economic

trends and developments will be published at an early date.

I am, dear Sir,

Yours faithfully,

WARNASENA RASAPUTRA

Governor.

Central Bank of Sri Lanka,

Colombo.

25th April, 1986.
