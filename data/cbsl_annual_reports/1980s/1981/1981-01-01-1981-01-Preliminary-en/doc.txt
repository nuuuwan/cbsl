Dear Sir,

In accordance with the provisions of Section 35 (1) of the Monetary Law

Act (Cap. 422), I submit herewith the Thirty Second Annual Report of the

Monetary Board of the Central Bank of Ceylon.

The report is in four parts. The first part reviews the performance of the

Sri Lanka economy in 1981, the problems that were encountered and the policies

and measures that were adopted. The second details the accounts and

operations of the Central Bank. Part three sets out the major administrative

measures adopted by the Monetary Board during 1981 ; and part four presents the

texts and extracts of major legislative enactments of 1981 relating to the functions

and operations of the Central Bank and banking institutions in Sri Lanka.

Since 1975, a more detailed review of the economic trends and developments

in the various sectors of the economy has been published separately under the title

"Review of the Economy ". The Review for 1981 will be published at an early

date.

I am, dear Sir,

Yours faithfully,

WARNASENA RASAPUTRAM

Governor,

Central Bank of Ceylon,

Colombo,

30th April, 1982.
