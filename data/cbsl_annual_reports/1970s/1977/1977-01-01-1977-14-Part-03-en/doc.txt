3 P E R S O N N E L

1. The Governor attended the following conferences during 1977:-

Twelfth SEACEN Governors' Conference in Malaysia (11-23 January). Directors' Meeting of the Asian Clearing Union in Karachi and official dis­cussions with the Reserve Bank of India (7-15 February).

Nineteenth Session of the Governing Body of the Asian Productivity Organiza­tion in Chiang Mai, Thailand, and official discussions with the Monetary Authority of Singapore (14-24 May.)

Commonwealth Central Bank Governors' Conference in London and the Annual Meeting of the Bank for International Settlements in Basle, Switzerland (1-21 June).

Annual Meetings of the I.M.F. and I.B.R.D. in Washington and official discussions with the Bank of England and commercial banks in London (14 September to 16 October).

Twelfth SEANZA Council of Governors Meeting in Seoul, South Korea (23 October to 3 November).

2. Mr. M. Rajendra, Appointed Member to the Monetary Board, ceased to be a member of the Monetary Board from August 11, 1977.

3. Mr. N. G. P. Panditharatne was appointed as the Appointed Member of the Monetary Board, with effect from August 11, 1977.

4. Mr. C. Chanmugam, Acting Secretary to the Ministry of Finance functioned as the official member of the Monetary Board during the absence of Dr. L. R. Jaya-wardena.

5. Dr. H. A. de S. Gunasekera, Secretary to the Ministry of Planning & Econo­mic Affairs ceased to be a member of the Monetary Board from November 28, 1977 with the reallocation of the functions of the Ministry of Planning and Economic Affairs.

6. Mr. D. R. Siriwardena acted as Secretary, Monetary Board, during the period of leave abroad of Mr. K. Kanagasabapathy.

7. Dr. Gamani Corea, Deputy Governor, continued to be on service with the UNCTAD as Secretary General.

8. Dr. W. M. Tilakaratna, Deputy Governor, was designated as Senior Deputy Governor with effect from 5th January, 1977. Dr. Tilakaratna functioned as Chief Executive Officer of the Bank in terms of Section 24 of the Monetary Law Act during the periods of absence abroad of the Governor.
