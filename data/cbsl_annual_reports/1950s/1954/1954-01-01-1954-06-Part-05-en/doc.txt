in table 10. Spot purchases were Rs. 455 -2 million (Rs. 150 -6 million in 1953)

and spot sales Rs. 342 -3 million (Rs. 640 -6 million in 1953). The value of contracts

entered into for forward purchases was Rs. 207 -3 million (Rs. 251 -9 million in 1953)

95. Bank Supervision.—The Department of Bank Supervision examined

eleven banks in 1954. Nine examinations were completed during the year.

96. Income and Expenditure.—The income of the Central Bank for the year

amounted to Rs. 11,509,135-75 and expenditure including allocations to reserves

in terms of section 38 of the Monetary Law Act amounted to Rs., 9,509,135-75.

The net profit of Rs. 2 million was paid and credited to the Consolidated Fund

in terms of section 39 (c) of the Act.
