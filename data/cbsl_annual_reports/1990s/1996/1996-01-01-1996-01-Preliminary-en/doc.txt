Your Excellency,

Section 35 ofthe Monetary Law Act (Chapter 422) requires the Monetary Board of

the Central Bank of Sri Lanka to submit an Annual Report to your Excellency on the state of

the economy, the condition of the Central Bank and a review of the policies and measures

adopted by the Monetary Board, within four months after the end of each financial year. The

forty seventh Annual Report of the Monetary Board of the Central Bank, in respect of the

Year 1996, is submitted herewith in fulfilment of this obligation.

Yours sincerely,
